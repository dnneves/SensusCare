How the Sensus Care App Works?

Why would you need Sensus Care?

  • People suffering from mood disorders can benefit from becoming aware of how they feel on everyday basis. For example, through mood tracking - and further relate this to: their use of medicine, daily hours of sleep, stress level and the use of stimulants or alcohol
  • Psychiatrists have a difficult time managing and keeping track of all their patients. This often happens due to busy schedules and a broad range of demands that make it difficult for them to give patients the care they require on a consistent basis.

    • Aims of the Sensus Care Platform:

  • Improve communication between psychiatrists and patients, and thus help psychiatrists make better informed decisions through frequent data collection, with easy access to patient history and progress monitoring
  • Help psychiatrists manage their workload by reducing the necessity of frequent meetings.
  • Help prevent patient relapses through automated alerts, notifying psychiatrists of patients being in a critical state
  • Keep history of patient reactions to different medicines

    • You Need Your Help!

    This much needed feedback will bring the team valuable information in order to continue the development of a platform for psychiatrists to remotely monitor their patients, with all the aforementioned benefits.

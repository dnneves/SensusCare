What is Sensus Care?

The Problem

We believe that the key to help people suffering from mood disorders and keep them healthy is to prevent relapses. This is done through counselling and medication alterations. The frequency with which patients meet and consult with psychiatrists is often not enough to prevent this. Furthermore, the data psychiatrists manage to extract during interviews with patients are often incomplete or not representative of the patient’s entire week/month/however long it’s been since last meeting.

Sensus Care is a remote patient monitoring platform for patients with mood disorders. We feel that psychiatrists and patients alike can benefit by more frequent ‘automated’ communication. We aim to achieve this via delivery of daily questionnaires to patients (through smartphones or the web) which aim to assess the patients’ mood. Our software attempts to determine whether the patient is stable and if not, deliver more questions to better understand the phase the patient is in.
